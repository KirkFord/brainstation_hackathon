"Purrfect Hunt 🐾"

How to Play:
Pick Your Challenge 🎯:
Choose your grid size (2 to 10). The bigger the grid, the harder the hunt!
Find the Cat 🐱:
Hidden among the colorful cubes is a sneaky cat! Can you find it? 🕵️‍♂️
If you find the cat within few clicks, you win! 🎉
Flip & Reveal 🔄:
Click on a cube to flip it over. If it’s a cat, you’ve won! If not, keep hunting.
No Cheating! 🤫:
Try to find the cat in as few clicks as possible or Ready to try again?
